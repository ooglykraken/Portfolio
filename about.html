About Me

My resume is right here.


I'm currently attending Indiana University for a Bachelor’s in Computer Science. My work includes Game Development at Plow Games/Plow Digital as well as helping Professor of Practice Mike Sellers in prototyping game ideas. He brought me along to help him with the development of Water Works in Fall '15 when the previous developer left. I named this website Intrepid Artisan because I think I recognize that the development and design of games is ever-changing so I mustn't be afraid of trying something new. In order to become a skilled Developer and Designer I must expose myself to as many experiences and opportunities as possible to develop these skills. My temerity also extends to my personal life. I'm an avid outdoorsman who's spent time canoeing in the Boundary Waters, backpacking through Alaska's Talkeetna mountains, and hiking a great deal closer to home here in Indiana. Though I tried most sports like football, soccer, rugby, tennis, and basketball, wrestling was the only one I enjoyed. I stuck with it from elementary to high school. After that I found myself drawn to boxing and jiu-jitsu or rather mixed martial arts in general. From all of this it would be fair to assume I enjoy thrill and challenge.


Games I'm playing right now: Path of Exile, Binding of Isaac: Afterbirth, Fallout 4

Games I really enjoyed: Banjo-Kazooie, Banjo-Tooie, KOTOR I & II, Monster Hunter Freedom Unite, Dark Souls I & II, Warcraft III, Castlevania: Symphony of the Night, Devil May Cry 3.
